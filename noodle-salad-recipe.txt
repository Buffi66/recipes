Ingredients:
 - 500 g butterfly noodles
 - 100 g pine nuts
 - 200 g feta cheese
 - 200 g sun-dried tomatoes
 - 30 leaves of basil
 - 50 ml olive oil
 - 2 garlic cloves
 - salt and pepper

Directions:
  1. cook noodles al dente
  2. cut tomatoes, feta cheese and garlic cloves
  3. roast pine nuts
  4. split leaves of basil
  5. put all in a bowl and add olive oil, salt and pepper
